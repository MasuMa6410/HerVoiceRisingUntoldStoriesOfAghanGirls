Her Voices Rising

Untold Stories of Aghan Girls

This platform shares the voices of Afghan girls whose stories are often unheard. Through safe and anonymous storytelling, we aim to highlight their experiences, challenges, and strengths.

Our goal is to create a space where real stories can be shared with dignity and respect, helping others better understand the realities of girls’ lives.

inspire & learn

Each story carries lessons. These reflections highlight what we can learn from the experiences, resilience, and strength of Afghan girls.

After analyzing collected stories, several common themes appeared:

  • Barriers to education
  • Social expectations
  • Lack of representation
  • Strong resilience

research & insights

This section presents key findings from our research, including patterns identified from stories and survey responses.

After analyzing collected stories, several common themes appeared:

  • Barriers to education
  • Social expectations
  • Lack of representation
  • Strong resilience

Survey results also showed that many people believe girls do not have enough opportunities to share their voices

understanding the system

The challenges faced by Afghan girls are connected to larger systems in society.

  • Education systems can limit opportunities
  • Social expectations influence behavior
  • Media often represents girls without their voices
  • Digital access affects participation

These systems shape how girls experience and express their lives.

ethics & safety

Our Responsibility

We are committed to protecting the safety and dignity of every individual. We ensure that all stories are shared with consent and respect for privacy. We also take care to avoid sensationalism and to present stories in a way that honors the experiences of those involved.

  • All stories are anonymized
  • No real names or exact locations are shared
  • Sensitive details are changed when necessary
  • Consent is required before sharing any story

Your safety matters.

about us & our team

We are a group of students working on a social change project focused on voice,representation, and equality.

Through this project, we aim to create a safe and respectful space where the real stories of Afghan girls can be shared and understood. By combining storytelling with research, we want to raise awareness, build empathy, and highlight both the challenges and strengths in their lives.
